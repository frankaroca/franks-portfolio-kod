About Franklin

>

I hail from Honduras, a Central American nation where I spent my formative years. From an early age, I exhibited a keen fascination for technology, a passion that catalyzed my initiation into the realm of software development during my tenure at the university within my homeland. In 2014, prompted by personal situations, I embarked on a journey that led me to relocate to the renowned region colloquially referred to as the "Heart of the Bay." Through unwavering diligence and an unrelenting commitment to my aspirations, I have persevered to attain the remarkable juncture at which I presently stand.
